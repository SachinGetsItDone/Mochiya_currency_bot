🍡 **Mochi Bot Features**

💰 **Economy**
• `cash` — Check coin balance
• `daily` — Claim daily coins (24h cooldown)
• `pay @user <amount>` — Send coins to a user
• `top [page]` — Global balance leaderboard
• `history [page]` — View recent transaction history
• `ping` — Check bot latency

🏅 **Tournament**
• `budget` — Check your tournament spending budget
• `players [page]` — List available players in the market
• `buy <player>` — Buy a player from the pool
• `roster [@user]` — View team roster & total value

🎲 **Match Betting**
• `matches [page]` — View open matches
• `matchinfo <id>` — Check match details & bet stats
• `support <player> <amount>` — Bet coins on a player (winnings split proportionally!)

🛒 **Season Shop**
• `shop [page]` — Browse items in the season shop
• `shopbuy <item>` — Buy an item using Mochi Coins
• `collection [@user]` — View inventory (`flex`)
• `gift @user <item>` — Gift an item from your collection
• `trade @user <your_item> for <their_item>` — Propose a trade
• `tradeaccept` / `tradedecline` — Accept/decline trade offers
• `tradecancel` — Cancel your pending trade offer

🔒 **Admin Only**
• `give @user <amount>` — Grant coins to a user
• `setbudget @user <amount>` — Set tournament budget
• `addplayer <name> <price>` — Add player to the pool
• `creatematch <playerA> vs <playerB> [OddsA] [OddsB]` — Open new match for betting (optional fixed odds)
• `endmatch <id> <winner>` — Set winner & distribute wins (supports set odds or proportional payouts)
• `cancelmatch <id>` — Cancel match & refund all bets
• `additem` — Add shop item (supports quoted names/descriptions)
• `removeitem <item>` — Soft-delete item (users keep purchased copies)
